How to Play Mastermind:

To win a game of Mastermind, the player must correctly guess the hidden code made up of the randomized placement of colored pegs. In this game the player has 6 possible colors selections and will need to get their placement and color correct within 8 turns. To start, simply click on the pegs to select their color, and then click on the board to place them within the rows, starting at one. To submit your guess of colors and placements on a row, use the check button! The white number indicates the number of pegs with the correct color and placement. The black number indicates the number of pegs with only the correct color.
